## Essential Islamic Terminology

### Religious Terms
- **Iman (Belief)**: Faith in heart and affirmation with tongue
- **Islam (Submission)**: Outward submission to Allah's will
- **Ihsan (Excellence)**: Worshipping Allah as if you see Him
- **Taqwa (God-consciousness)**: Piety and awareness of Allah
- **Deen (Religion)**: Complete way of life

### Theological Terms
- **Tawhid**: Divine oneness
- **Shirk**: Polytheism; associating partners with Allah
- **Qadar**: Divine decree; predestination
- **'Adl**: Divine justice
- **Rahmah**: Mercy

### Legal Terms
- **Halal**: Permissible
- **Haram**: Forbidden
- **Mubah**: Neutral; permissible
- **Wajib**: Obligatory
- **Makruh**: Disliked

### Scholarly Terms
- **Ijma'**: Scholarly consensus
- **Qiyas**: Analogical reasoning
- **Ijtihad**: Independent legal reasoning
- **Madhab**: School of law
- **Fatwa**: Legal ruling

### Spiritual Terms
- **Dhikr**: Remembrance of Allah
- **Du'a**: Supplication/prayer
- **Salah**: The five daily prayers
- **Sawm**: Fasting
- **Hajj**: Pilgrimage
